Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to advertise white water rafting, specifically from Dry Oar, to the audience, for the sake of revenue. We are building revenue for the White Water Rafting Company. We share information concerning the product offered from this company, namely, our white water rafting experience.

Audience

The audience would be American, though international visitors are also welcome. They would be ideally between their early twenties and sixties, so that they'd have both the needed physical fitness and enough money to pay for our product. This intended audience enjoys outdoor activities, such as white water rafting. Their needs would be stress relief from the every-day grind. This site gives them an opportunity to explore and consider our product for such stress-relief. This site will most likely be accessed through desktop.
