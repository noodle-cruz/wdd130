General Experience & Reflections

The most challenging part of this project was any of the scripting or javascript. We only saw a one-day brief overview of how that can look like, but we never had to actually code anything ourselves. I had ask AI for these things (which I don't really like having to do). If I could do the project again, I'd do it with knowledge on how to do the scripting or javascript. I feel a little more confident in working on real-world projects. There were times when things went well and when things didn't work well, so now I know somewhat of what to expect for real-world project.
